About Feed a Life

Feed a Life is a platform dedicated to tackling two critical issues simultaneously: food waste and hunger. We connect restaurants and food businesses that have excess food with organizations and individuals who need it.

Every day, tons of perfectly good food goes to waste while millions go hungry. We're bridging this gap by making it easy for restaurants to donate their surplus food and for those in need to find available meals nearby.

How It Works

For Restaurants

  1. Register your restaurant
  2. Post available surplus food
  3. Set pickup time and location
  4. Confirm pickups and track impact
Register Restaurant

For NGOs & Organizations

  1. Create an account
  2. Find available food near you
  3. Request food pickup
  4. Distribute to those in need
Register Organization

For Individuals

  1. No account needed
  2. Find food available near you
  3. Contact the restaurant
  4. Pickup food during specified times
