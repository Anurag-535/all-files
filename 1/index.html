About Feed a Life

Feed a Life is a community-driven platform with a mission to reduce food waste and address hunger. We connect restaurants that have surplus food with individuals and organizations who can put it to good use.

Each year, billions of pounds of food are wasted while millions of people go hungry. Our platform provides a simple solution to this complex problem by creating connections within local communities.

Whether you're a restaurant owner looking to reduce waste, an NGO serving the community, or an individual in need, Feed a Life is here to help make a difference, one meal at a time.
